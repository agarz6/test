Strawberry Fluff

This chicken asparagus pasta is really good and also very healthy for you and your family. If you don't like something that is in the ingredients, you can always change the ingredients to how you like it.

Prep time: 5mins

Cook time: 5mins

Total time: 10mins

Serving Size: 6

Ingredients

  • 12 oz whipped cream
  • 8 oz Condensed milk
  • choped pineapple or sliced
  • strawberry filling
  • nuts (optional)

Direction

  1. Put the whipped cream in a large container and add the condensed milk and mix throughly.

  2. When you are finished mixing, add the strawberry filling and the pineapple.

  3. When it is mixed well, you can put nuts on top and then put it in the freezers to get hard.
